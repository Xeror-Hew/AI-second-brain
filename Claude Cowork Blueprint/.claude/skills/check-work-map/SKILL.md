---
name: check-work-map
description: Check the work map before exploring or changing an unfamiliar part of the work.
when_to_use: ALWAYS before you explore, navigate, or change a part of the work you don't know yet this session, ahead of reading files loose
---

Before you explore or change work you don't know:

1. Open `project_brain/work_map/map_index.md` for the top-level view and the fragment table.
2. Find the fragment whose description matches what you need and read it.
3. Then go to the files (use `path:line` when it helps you navigate).

No map, or clearly stale? Run `/map` first. The map gets you to the right part faster, then you open the files.
